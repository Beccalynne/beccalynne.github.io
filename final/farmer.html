Meet the Farmer

Dane is the founder of Wholesome Harvest. He has a masters in teaching and taught for three years before discovering a passion for farming. Dane applies his love for learning in researching and learning everything he can about farming and how to improve the farm. Dane and his wife Jenni have been married for 9 years. They have two children who can be seen running around the farm and helping with the animals.

Some Friendly Faces Around the Farm...

James

James is an extra hand on the farm and is out there quite often. He is currently attending BYU Idaho and working on his Bachelors degree in Computer Engineering. He enjoys spending time outside, riding motorcycles, fishing, and losing fantasy football leagues. James enjoys being a handy man working in automotive and construction so you will see him working on tractors and odd jobs around the farm. He loves his diesel truck and spending time with family.

Gabi

Gabi works at the farm managing the greenhouse and garden area. She loves to grow anything that she can get her hands on. Gabi will be graduating here shortly from BYU Idaho with her Bachelors degree in Horticulture. She will be working on the farm to bring new ideas to life. She loves doing anything crafty and her favorite thing to do is spend time with her friends and family!
