Typography

Heading Font: Sans-Serif

This an easy to read font for headings and so the audience can see the different amenities offered and can search a specific one if they are looking for something in particular. It also matches the font for his other business website so there is some congruency.

Paragraph Font: Open Sans Condensed

It is a little more informal and not bolded to not take away from the heading. Even though it is less formal it is still easily read to those of all ages.
