Typography

Heading Font: Courier Prime

This is a modern font that also captures the eye. It is easy to read and easily find the section of information that you need.

Paragraph Font: Work Sans

I like the contrast in fonts to the heading, due to being a Sans. Each letter is also spread out enough that it's a little easier to read in my opinion.
